Our Story & Mission

Founded by Yash Sharma, Yash Dairy began with a single vision: reviving absolute purity inside local households. Disappointed by chemical additives and heavily processed industrial choices in modern packaging markets, we stepped back to basics.

We raise and nourish cattle under happy structural layouts with strict nutrient-loaded natural diets. Located prominently near Itwa High School, our operational methods focus perfectly on sustainability, freshness, and high quality checking parameters to provide healthy products you completely trust.
